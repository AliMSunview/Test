Using the Home Page

The ChangeGear Self-Service Portal page is a portal to the services ChangeGear offers on the Web. You can quickly access important information and links regarding the incident requests you have made. It can also offer a quick self-service area to Request Help (create an incident), search tickets, view service announcements, and look up Knowledge Base articles to troubleshoot services.

Note: The Self-Service Portal may appear different than the following screenshot depending on your ChangeGear configuration and role privileges.

  1. The Search text box allows you to search a variety of ChangeGear modules including Incidents and the Knowledge Base.

  2. The Announcements section highlights the published/scheduled announcements derived from the Announcement Portal.

  3. The Help and Request Buttons allow you to submit requests, view the Knowledge Base or contact support.

  4. The Popular Solutions section shows published articles on the Knowledge Base that have received top ratings from users.

  5. The Request Views section shows various views of your help, service, and change requests.

  6. The Resources sections links you to various internal and third-party websites.
