Viewing Your Tickets

The View menu, located on the grid's toolbar, allows you to select unique ways to view the information in the grid, including pre-filtering or sorting only the items that you wish to view. The View menu also allows you to save customized views in addition to the views from the group menus.

The group menus side-panel has four common view menus: My Views, My Team Views, Global Views, and Other. The Teams view may not appear in the side-panel if your ChangeGear administrator has not enabled Teams. The Change Module may have an additional My Tasks menu.

  • The My Views menu displays tickets that are associated with you. You have either created, requested, or have been assigned to the tickets. The My Views menu in the Change Module also includes views for tickets you must approve or have approved. If you create a custom view, your view displays in your My Views menu.

  • The My Team Views menu displays tickets that are associated with the team(s) of which you are a member. The ChangeGear administrator controls both the membership of teams and enabling teams.

  • The Global Views menu displays any ticket that you can view.

  • The Other menu displays useful locations, such as the Self-Service Portal, Announcement Portal, Announcement Manager, or Template Manager. Not all these options may be available to you depending on both your role in ChangeGear and your ChangeGear license.
