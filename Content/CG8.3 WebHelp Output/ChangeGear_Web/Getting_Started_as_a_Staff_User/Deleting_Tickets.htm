Deleting Tickets

On some occasions, it is necessary to delete a ticket. For example, when you decide a new or an unsubmitted ticket should not be submitted, you can delete the ticket.

Note: By default, only administrators can delete tickets after submission. Other users can be granted the delete privilege in the administration section. Ticket Requesters, however, cannot use delete privilege and can delete only unsubmitted tickets.

To delete a ticket:

  1. Navigate to the appropriate module.

  2. Select the ticket that needs to be deleted and click Delete .

  3. You are prompted to confirm the deletion, click OK.
