Using the Suggestion Center

The Suggestion Center provides suggestions related to the current ticket or the user entered information for a new ticket. Like the Field Recommender, the Suggestion Center is a self-learning process that uses your input and feedback to intelligently find and suggest similar items from the configured data sources which can be one of the modules like Incident, Change or Knowledge Base.

Note: You may not have access to this feature depending on your ChangeGear installation.

To use the Suggestion Center:

  1. Open a new ticket. The Suggestion Center icon  appears on the right-hand side of the ticket form.

  2. Enter a value in the Summary field. As you type, ChangeGear searches to find related tickets and Knowledge Base articles. If a match is found, the icon lights up .

    Note: If the icon does not appear, then ChangeGear could not find any matches using the entered text.

  3. Click on the Suggestion Center icon to open the Suggestion Center. The Suggestion Center lists similar tickets from the current module, as well as Knowledge Base articles related to the issue. Both tickets and articles are ordered according to how relevant the item is to the current ticket.

    Tip: For more information on how relevance is measured, see Confidence Levels in the Using the Field Recommender topic.

  4. Click on the summary to display more information about the item. For tickets, the Suggestion Center displays the resolution field of the ticket. For articles, the Suggestion Center displays the body of the article.

  5. Click on either the thumbs up or thumbs down icon to rate the relevancy of the article or ticket. Thumbs up means the item was helpful for this particular issue, and thumbs down means the item was not helpful.

  6. Click on the Actions button to open a list of actions that you can perform on the article or ticket.

    1. For Knowledge Base articles, you can perform the following actions:

      • View KB Article opens the suggested article in a new window.

      • Resolve With resolves the ticket and provides the summary of the KB article in the resolution text.

    2. For tickets, you can perform the following actions:

      • View Ticket opens the suggested ticket in a new window.

      • Copy Resolution copies the ticket's resolution text to your clipboard. You can then paste the resolution into another ticket.

      • Resolve With uses the resolution provided in a different ticket to resolve and close the current ticket.

  7. Click the close button to close the Suggestion Center.
