Incident Workflow Status Bar

ChangeGear uses a graphical representation to show the progression of the incident through the incident management process. This work flow status bar tracks the entire process from the submission of an incident (New) throughout the life cycle until the incident is successfully closed (Closed).

Click on the options below to learn more about the workflow status bar:

  • New

  • In-Progress

  • Pending Close

  • Closed
