Exporting Solutions to Articles

If you frequently reuse the text of a resolution, link the Incident to other tickets, or if the resolution details a workaround, the resolution may be a good candidate for a KB article.

Note: To create a KB article from an Incident resolution, you must have the appropriate privileges in the Knowledge Base.

To export a resolution to the Knowledge Base:

  1. In the Incident form, open the Resolution tab.

  2. Under the Resolution field, click Create KB Article.

  3. A new window opens for the KB article.

  4. Enter any relevant information. The Body field is populated from the Resolution field.

  5. Check the Public box if you want the article to be viewable by all users. Clear the check mark if you only want to distribute the article to other staff users.

  6. Enter keywords appropriate to the topic of the article in the Keywords field. The keywords are used as search terms.

  7. To send the article to the selected reviewer, click Request Review.

  8. To complete the article process, click Publish.
