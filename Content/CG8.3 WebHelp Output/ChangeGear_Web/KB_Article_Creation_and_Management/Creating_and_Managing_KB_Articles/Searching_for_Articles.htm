Searching for Articles

To search for an article using the KB Search feature:

  1. Open the Knowledge Base module from the ChangeGear primary navigation menu.

  2. Enter your search terms in the search box. Make sure you have selected the proper view to search, Recent Articles or All Articles.

  3. Click Search.

  4. All the articles found relating to the search terms are displayed.
