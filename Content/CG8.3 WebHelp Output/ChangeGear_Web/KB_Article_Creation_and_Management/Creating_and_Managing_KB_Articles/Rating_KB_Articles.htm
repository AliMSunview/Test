Rating Articles

You can rate published KB articles based on their helpfulness. ChangeGear uses a five-point rating system, where one point means the article was not helpful and five points means the article was very helpful. Depending on your ChangeGear configuration, you can also view the average of all submitted ratings for each article.

Note: If you are unable to see rating information, please contact your ChangeGear administrator.

Submitting a Rating

ChangeGear offers two ways to rate KB articles: a star-based rating system and a thumbs up/thumbs down-based rating system. Only one system can be active at a time, and the system that is available to you depends on your ChangeGear configuration. Once you have submitted a rating, you will not be able to change it.

To submit a rating using the star-based rating system:

  1. Open the article in ChangeGear Web.

    Tip: Staff users can rate articles directly from the article editor.

  2. Click on a star representing the article's helpfulness. One star means the article was not helpful, and five stars means the article was very helpful.

  3. Click Submit.

To submit a rating using the thumbs-based rating system:

  1. Open the article in ChangeGear Web.

  2. Click on either the thumbs up or thumbs down icon.

    Tip: A thumbs-up is the same as a five-star rating, and a thumbs-down is the same as a one-star rating.

  3. Click OK when the confirmation dialog appears.

Once you submit a rating, the page will refresh. Your previous rating will be displayed in place of the rating submission control.

Note: Rating an article will not update the Modified or Modified By fields. However, resetting the rating will.

Resetting an Article's Rating

Staff users can reset the average rating of an article back to 0 as long as the article is in the Draft or Unreviewed state.

To reset an article's rating:

  1. Open the article in ChangeGear Web.

  2. Click the Reset link next to the average rating.

  3. Click OK when the confirmation dialog appears.

Once you confirm the reset, the page will refresh. The average rating will now be 0.
