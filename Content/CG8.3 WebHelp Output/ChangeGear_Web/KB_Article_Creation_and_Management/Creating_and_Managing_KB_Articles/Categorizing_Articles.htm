Categorizing Articles

You can organize KB articles by using categories. A category is a collection of KB articles that share a common topic or theme. Once a category is created by a ChangeGear administrator, users with the correct privileges can assign KB articles to the category through the article editor.

In addition to organizing KB articles, categories can be used to assign articles to teams. When a category is assigned to a team, only the users in that team can view the articles in that category.

Note: You can only categorize articles if you have the correct privileges. If you don't have the correct privileges, the drop-down appears as a read-only text box. Please contact your ChangeGear administrator to have these privileges set.

To categorize a KB article:

  1. Open the article in ChangeGear Web.

  2. Select a category from the Category drop-down. If the drop-down is read-only or empty, you may not have permission to assign categories.

    Tip: Use caution when assigning categories. If you assign a category that you don't have access to, you may no longer be able to view the article.

  3. Click to save the article.
