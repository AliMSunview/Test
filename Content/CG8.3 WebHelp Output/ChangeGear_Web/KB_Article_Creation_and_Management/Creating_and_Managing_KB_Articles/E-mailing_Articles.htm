E-mailing Articles

You can easily and quickly share information with colleagues inside or outside of your organization using ChangeGear's built-in e-mail function. The e-mail function automatically opens your default e-mail client with the link to the article already in the body. You can send an e-mail directly from an open KB article or you can e-mail the article from the related request.

To e-mail articles from the KB module:

  1. Select the article from the KB module or open an article.

  2. Click E-mail. Your default mail program opens.

  3. Enter the e-mail address of the person to whom you want to send the link to the KB article.

  4. Send the e-mail.

To e-mail articles from an incident or problem:

  1. Select the incident or problem to which you are responding.

  2. Open the Related Items tab.

  3. Select the KB article and click E-mail

    - OR -

    Link to the KB article and e-mail the article.

To e-mail articles from the Print Preview:

  1. Open the KB article from the Self-Service Portal

    - OR -

    Open the KB article and click Print.

  2. Click E-mail on the toolbar.
