Accessing Total Views

ChangeGear tracks the total number of unique views per article, allowing you to see which articles are being consumed the most. Each view tracks not only the user who viewed the article, but also their role when they viewed the article. When combined with rating controls, view tracking gives you a comprehensive view of an article's popularity and effectiveness.

Depending on your ChangeGear configuration, you may be able to see the total view count by viewing or editing a KB article. You can also access an article's total view count through a view.

Note: Views only count for published articles.

To add the total view count to a view:

  1. Open the Knowledge Base module.

  2. Click the Manage View drop-down.

  3. Click Show/Hide Columns....

  4. In the Field Chooser, click the TotalViews field to add it to the view.

  5. Optionally, click and drag the TotalViews field to reposition it in the view.
