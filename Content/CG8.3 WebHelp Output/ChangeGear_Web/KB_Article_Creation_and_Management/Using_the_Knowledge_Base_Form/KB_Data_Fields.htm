KB Data Fields

When you create a Knowledge Base article, enter the Title and Body before sending the article for review or publishing. The Body can be formatted like a word processing document by using the formatting bars on top of the body field. You can add attachments to the KB using the Attachments tab.

You can also add Keywords to facilitate searches and select when the article is published to all your users.
