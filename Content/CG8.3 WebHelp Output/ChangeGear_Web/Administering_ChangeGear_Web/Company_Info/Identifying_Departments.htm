Identifying Departments

Department information identifies departments within your organization.

  1. On the Web Administration page, click Platform > Company Info > Department. ChangeGear displays the Departments screen.
  2. To add a new department, click the plus sign   in the upper right corner. ChangeGear opens a row in the grid so you can enter the department  Name and Description.

Note: You can add more columns to your grid to add optional department information: Created, Department ID, Editor Layout, Modified, Phone, and Department  Owner.

  1. To edit an existing department's information, hover over the line and click the pencil on the right. ChangeGear opens that row in the grid so you can change the information.
  2. To delete a department from the grid hover and click the trashcan icon on the right.
  3. When you have finished adding or editing department information, click the save button or the right.
