Specifying Company Information

The Company Info option in the Web Administration module allows you to set up information about your company.

  1. On the Web Administration page under Platform, click Company Info > Companies. ChangeGear displays the Companies screen.
  2. To add a new company, click the plus sign   in the upper right corner. ChangeGear opens a row in the grid so you can enter the company Name, Contact, and Notes.

Note: You can add more columns to your grid to add optional company information: Address 1, Address 2, City, Created, Editor Layout, Integration Account ID, Modified, Phone #, State/Province, WebUrl, Zip code, Accessed By, and Created By.

  1. To edit an existing company's information, hover over the line and click the pencil on the right. ChangeGear opens that row in the grid so you can change the information.
  2. To delete a company from the grid hover and click the trashcan icon on the right.
  3. When you have finished adding or editing company information, click the save button or the right.
