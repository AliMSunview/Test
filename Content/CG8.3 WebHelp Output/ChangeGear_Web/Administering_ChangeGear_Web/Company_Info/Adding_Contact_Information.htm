Adding Contact Information

Contact information identifies details used to notify external users.

  1. On the Web Administration page, click Platform > Company Info > Contact. ChangeGear displays the Contacts screen.
  2. To add a new contact, click the plus sign   in the upper right corner. ChangeGear opens a row in the grid so you can enter the contact's First  Name, Last Name, and Title.

Note: You can add more columns to your grid to add optional contact information: Created, Editor Layout, E-mail, Extension, Fax, Modified, Name, Phone, Accessed By, Created By, and  Modified By.

  1. To edit an existing contact's information, hover over the line and click the pencil on the right. ChangeGear opens that row in the grid so you can change the information.
  2. To delete a contact from the grid hover and click the trashcan icon on the right.
  3. When you have finished adding or editing contact information, click the save button or the right.
