Overview of Team Management

The Team Manager module enables you to perform team maintenance for teams previously created in the ChangeGear Desktop Client application. You can perform routine team member maintenance and modify team profile information. This team maintenance is offered in addition to the Administration Team Management functions provided in the ChangeGear Desktop Client.

Based on your role privileges, you can edit team information for ANY team or ONLY teams owned by you, (i.e., you are the Team Owner).

Note: You may not have access to this ChangeGear feature depending on your permissions.
