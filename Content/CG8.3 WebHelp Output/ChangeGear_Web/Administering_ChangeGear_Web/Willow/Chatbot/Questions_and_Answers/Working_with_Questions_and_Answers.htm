Working with Questions and Answers

Questions and  Answers are always specific to a single intent and not shared. You can review and edit questions or answers in Willow chatbot.

  1. Click Willow > Chatbot > Questions and Answers. ChangeGear displays the Questions and Answers (Willow) screen. From here, you can add new questions or answers, select columns to show in the grid, filter the grid, and refresh the page. You can also drill down on individual questions or answers to edit (change their intent, type or text) or delete them.

  1. To edit a question or answer, highlight its row, and click the pencil icon on the right.  ChangeGear opens the in-line edit row.


  1. Click the Intent field to change the assigned intent, the Type (Question or Answer), or the Text field to change the wording of the question or answer. Click the save icon to save your changes.
  2. To delete a question or answer, select its row and click the trashcan icon to the right. ChangeGear asks, Are you sure you want to delete this record? When you click YES ChangeGear deletes it.
  3. Once you have finished making changes, you need to retrain the model to get a new ML model and test it. See Implementing Changes to a Model.
