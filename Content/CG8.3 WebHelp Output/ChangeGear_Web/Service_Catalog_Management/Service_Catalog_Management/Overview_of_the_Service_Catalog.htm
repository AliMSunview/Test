Overview of the Service Catalog

The Service Catalog is a web portal that helps users find and request services. Users can search for services, browse by categories, read service descriptions and requirements, and request services. A submitted service request may create tickets in other ChangeGear modules, or it can link to other resources, such as intranet pages or third-party vendor systems.  The Service Catalog can be configured with additional information, including widgets that list knowledge base articles, announcements, or a user's current service requests.    


    
Note: ChangeGear instances upgraded from older versions of ChangeGear will have two Service Catalog configurations available: Classic and New. This help document identifies when functionality differs between the two versions. The image above is an example of the new Service Catalog. 

The new Service Catalog can be configured online using a drag-and-drop interface.    

The classic Service Catalog can also be configured online.
