Relationships Tab

The Relationships Tab records how resources are related to other resources. You can use the plus button to view how other resources are related to the resource and the minus button to collapse the information tree. You can also add, edit, or delete relationships between CIs.

Relationships can be added by clicking the Add button, choosing a related CI, and then choosing the relationship between the CIs. A CI can have multiple relationships. In the example image, the VULCAN Firewall is a parent of the HERMES Cisco Router.

To relate CIs:

  1. Click Add. The Related Resource Chooser dialog displays.

  2. Select the CI you want to add a relationship.

  3. Click Add. The Select Relation Type dialog displays.

  4. Choose how this CI is related to your original CI in the Relation Type field.

  5. Click OK.

  6. The selected CI appears in the Selected Items box.

    Note: You can add multiple CIs.

  7. After you are done relating CIs to the original CI, click OK.

To edit an existing relationship:

  1. In the tree view of the Relationship tab, select the CI whose relationship you want to modify.

  2. Click Edit. The Select Relationship Type dialog displays.

  3. Choose the appropriate role. For example, if you're changing the role of a router and a server, the server's role could be Attached to or Child of or Connected to the router. After you choose one role, the other role fills in with the appropriate relationship.

  4. Click OK.

To remove an existing relationship:

  1. In the tree view of the Relationship tab, select the CI whose relationship you want to delete.

  2. Click Remove.

  3. In the confirmation prompt, select OK.
