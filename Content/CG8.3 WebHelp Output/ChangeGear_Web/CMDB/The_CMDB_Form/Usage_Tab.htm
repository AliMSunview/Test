Usage Tab

The Usage tab stores information related to who owns the resource, which business services use the resource, who uses the resource, and which policies apply to the resource.

Owners

  • Owner: Use the drop-down menu to select the appropriate user. The resource owner is usually a department head or manager who has purchased the CI.

  • Co-owner: Use the drop-down menu to select the appropriate user. The co-owner is usually the primary system user or an alternate contact to the owner of the CI.

  • Tech-owner: Use the drop-down menu to select the appropriate user. The technical owner is usually the person who administers the CI.

  • Team: Use the drop-down menu to select the appropriate user. The team owner is usually the team that manages or administers the CI.

  • Users/Groups: Use the button to add users or groups who rely on this CI. This field is used to automatically populate the Impacted Users field when the CI is selected as an impacted resource or business service.

Policies

  • Compliance Policy: Displays the Compliance Policies associated with the CI.

  • Disaster Recovery: Displays the Disaster Recovery plans associated with the CI.

  • Security: Displays the Security Policies associated with the CI.

  • SLA: Displays the Service Level Agreements associated with the CI.

Business Services

The Business Services section is automatically populated by relationships made in the Relationships tab in the Desktop client. For example, if you had a large mainframe with executing several business services, such as accounting and payroll services, the mainframe's business services would include the accounting service and the payroll service. The relationship between the mainframe and its business services must have been properly defined in the Relationships tab in the Desktop Client.
