Service History Tab

The Service History tab displays incidents, problems, and RFCs logged against a CI as well as any changes or modifications recorded in the CMDB for the CI. Tickets that impact the CI are displayed under Related Service Items, if you have purchased ChangeGear's Change Management Solution or Service Desk. Changes made to the CI record are recorded under CI History. Tracked assets cannot be added to RFCs, and do not display any change requests. The image below shows the Service History tab with the Service Desk.

If you have the Change Management module or Service Desk, you can directly edit tickets from this tab.

To edit a Service Item:

  1. Select the appropriate item.

  2. Click Edit in the Related Service Items group. The ticket form will display.

    Note: You may have to re-enter your password.

  3. Edit the ticket.

  4. Save the ticket.
