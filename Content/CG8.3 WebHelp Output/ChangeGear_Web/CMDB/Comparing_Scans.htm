Comparing Scans

When a CI has been scanned, ChangeGear creates a historical record of the scanned configuration. That scan history record can be used to compare the configuration of a CI at various points in the CI's scan history. ChangeGear can either compare a scan to a pre-selected baseline, or it can compare two scans against each other.

A baseline in ChangeGear refers to a scan history record that the CMDB administrator determines accurately reflects the official configuration against which all new scans are compared. The baseline can be switched to a different scan history record or removed from the CI entirely.

To set the baseline:

  1. Select the appropriate scan history record

  2. Click Set as Baseline.

By selecting a single scan history record and clicking Difference, ChangeGear displays any differences between the selected scan and the baseline, when the baseline has been set.

To compare a scan to the baseline:

  1. Select the scan.

  2. Click Difference.

If you select two scan history records, the records are compared against each other instead of the baseline.

The baseline can be switched quickly between different scan history records without having to remove the baseline and reapply it.

To switch a baseline:

  1. Select the new scan history record.

  2. Click Set as Baseline.

Should you wish to no longer have a baseline for your CI, you can easily remove it.

To remove a baseline:

  1. Select your baseline.

  2. Click Remove Baseline.
