Viewing Dependencies

The Resource Map presents a graphical view of related resources. The CIs displayed in the Map View are controlled by which CIs are related to the selected CI. The direction of the arrows indicate the flow of impact. For example, if Apollo fails, then HR Services business service will also fail. Tracked assets and unclassified items are not displayed in the dependency map.

If you want to view or edit the CI's details, you can double-click on the resource and the CI form displays.

Toolbar

  • View Opens the CI form of the selected CI.

  • Zoom Decrease or increase the magnification of the map.

  • Show Roles Displays the relationships between CIs.

  • Level For the selected CI, choose how many levels of related CIs are displayed on the dependency map. The first level displays CIs that are directly related to the selected CI. The second level displays both the CIs that are directly related and the CIs that are related to the first level and so forth.
