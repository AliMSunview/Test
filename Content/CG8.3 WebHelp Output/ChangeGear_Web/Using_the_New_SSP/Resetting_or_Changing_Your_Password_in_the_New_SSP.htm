Resetting or Changing Your Password in the New SSP

You can reset your ChangeGear password only when you are a ChangeGear Authenticated user. Generally, ChangeGear Authenticated users are those that have a user name and password specific for logging in to ChangeGear. Please contact your IT department or ChangeGear Administrator if you have questions about your account.

Note: If you are not a ChangeGear Authenticated user and try to reset your password using the link on the ChangeGear Login screen, you will receive an error and your password will not be reset.

To reset your password:

  1. In a Web browser, open the login page.

  2. Click on the link I forgot my password. The Password Reset dialog displays.

  3. Enter the e-mail address that is connected to your account. This account is the e-mail account that receives messages from ChangeGear.

  4. Click Submit.

  5. An e-mail is sent to your e-mail address with your new password.
