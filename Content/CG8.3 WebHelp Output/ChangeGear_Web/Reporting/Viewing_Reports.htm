Viewing Reports

ChangeGear provides several categories of predefined reports based on your data. Before you use a report, review it to ensure the report provides the information you require.

To view a report:

  1. Click Reports in the ChangeGear main navigation menu.

  2. Choose the appropriate category and subcategory from the list.

  3. To view your report, double-click the report title

    - OR -

    Select the report and click the View button.
