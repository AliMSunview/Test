Viewing Known Errors

Known Errors can be viewed from three different sections of ChangeGear:

  • The search option in the ticket form,

  • the link dialog in the Related Items tab,

  • and the Known Error view in the Problem module.

To view Known Errors from the ticket search option or link dialog:

  1. In an open ticket, click the search button

    -OR-

    Open the Related Items and click Link.

  2. Open the Error tab.

  3. Search for your search term.

  4. Select a Known Error.

  5. Click View.

To view Known Errors in the problem module:

  1. Open the problem module.

  2. In the group menu, click on Known Errors

    -OR-

    In the views menu, select Known Errors.
