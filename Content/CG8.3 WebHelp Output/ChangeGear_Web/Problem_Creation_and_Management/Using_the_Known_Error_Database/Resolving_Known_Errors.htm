Resolving Known Errors

Resolve a Known Error when the root cause of the error has been corrected or the designated fix has been applied. The known error will then enter the pending close state.

To resolve a known error:

  1. Open the Problem module.

  2. Open the Known Error you wish to resolve.

  3. In the toolbar, click Resolve Error.
