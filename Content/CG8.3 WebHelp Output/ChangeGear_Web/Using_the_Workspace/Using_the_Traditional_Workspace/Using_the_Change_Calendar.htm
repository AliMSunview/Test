Using the Change Calendar

The Change Calendar provides a monthly view of change requests organized by date. By default, it displays a timeline view of requests according to their start and end dates. It can also display a summary of events grouped by due date.

The Change Calendar only displays tickets that are open and have been approved. Tickets that are unapproved or pending approval will not appear on the calendar. Closed tickets are also not shown by default, but you can include them through the Change Calendar Settings dialog (see the Customizing the Change Calendar section below).

Note: This feature is only available to users in the Change Staff role. You may not have access to this feature depending on your permissions or ChangeGear edition.

To open the Change Calendar:

  1. Navigate to the Workspace from the ChangeGear primary navigation menu.

  2. Click the Change Calendar icon on the right side of the screen.

Navigating the Change Calendar

When the Change Calendar is first opened, it shows the current month with today's date highlighted. You can navigate to a different month by clicking the Previous or Next buttons located above the day view. If you wish to return to the current month, press the This Month button.

If a date has tickets associated with it (as indicated by a number circled in red), clicking on that date displays a drop-down menu with the ticket ID, status, summary, and due time of each ticket. Clicking on the ticket opens the ticket form in a new window. To close the drop-down menu, click on the date again.

To close the Change Calendar, click the button in the top-right corner of the screen.

Customizing the Change Calendar

The Change Calendar Settings dialog lets you specify how tickets are categorized and displayed in the Change Calendar. You can configure the following settings:

  • Categorize by location limits the tickets shown to a specific location.

    Tip: Locations are configured in the ChangeGear Desktop Client. For more information, see the Configuring Lookup Lists section of the ChangeGear Administrator's Guide.

  • Categorize by team limits the tickets shown to a specific team. This includes Notification teams and Security teams. Select All to display tickets from all teams.

    Tip: The team assigned to a ticket is determined by the ticket's ItemOwner field.

  • Categorize by type applies color-coding to the tickets shown based on the field selected. You can choose between the Priority, Impact, and Urgency fields.

  • Categorize by date determines which date fields are used when displaying tickets. When Planned Start and End Dates is selected, tickets are displayed as a span between the dates in the ticket's Planned Start and Planned Complete fields. When Due Date is selected, each ticket is displayed on the date corresponding to its Due Date field.

  • Include Closed Tickets toggles whether tickets in the Closed status are displayed on the calendar.

To customize the Change Calendar:

  1. Open the Change Calendar.

  2. Open the Change Calendar Settings dialog by clicking the button in the top-right corner of the Change Calendar.

  3. Make your changes.

  4. Click OK to save your changes and close the dialog.
