Customizing the Workspace

Similar to the Dashboard, the Workspace provides a canvas where you can add, edit, delete, re-size, arrange, and zoom into widgets. Any customizations made will only apply to your personal Workspace.

To add a widget to the Workspace:

  1. Workspace module from the ChangeGear primary navigation menu.

  2. Based on your privileges, you may have to select My Workspace from the list of layouts (personas) located in the workspace menu.

  3. Click the Add Widget button in the Workspace menu. A list of Widget Types displays.

  4. Select a Widget Type to add the widget to the Workspace.

    Note: The Score Card, My Views, and My Links widgets require additional setup before they can be used. When added, they display "Setup Widget" as shown in the image below. To configure these widgets, perform the steps under the Editing Widgets section below.

To edit a widget:

  1. Place your mouse cursor over the widget. A series of icons appears in the top-right corner of the widget.

  2. Press the Settings button to open the Settings dialog. Depending on the type of widget selected, different options will appear in the dialog.

    Note: The Activities and My Reports widgets have fixed settings.

    • For a My Links widget, enter the Display Text and the Universal Resource Locator (URL) of the link. The URL specifies the location that ChangeGear will direct you to when you click the link. This can be an external web site or another location in ChangeGear Web.

    • For a Score Card widget, select the Module that you want to retrieve data from, then select the Chart that you wish to display. A Chart corresponds to a data point, such as the number of active incidents or the number of change requests pending approval.

    • For a My Views widget, select the Module that you want to retrieve data from, then select the View Section that you want to display views from. The View Section specifies the set of views that the widget will display. You can select from My Views, My Team Views, and Global Views categories.

      Tip: You don't need to specify http:// when entering a website. However, you should specify https:// when accessing a secure website.

  3. Click Save to save your changes

    - OR -

    Click Close to close the dialog without saving.

To delete a widget:

  1. Place your mouse cursor over the widget. A series of icons appears in the top-right corner of the widget.

  2. Click the Delete button. A confirmation dialog prompts you to make sure you want to delete this widget.

  3. Click OK.

To resize a widget:

  1. Move your mouse cursor over the bottom right corner of the widget until you see a diagonal double arrow .

  2. Click and drag the widget to resize it.

To move a widget:

  1. Click and hold your cursor on the widget.

  2. Drag and drop the widget to the area where you want it placed.

To zoom in on a widget:

  1. Place your mouse cursor over the widget. A series of icons appears in the top-right corner of the widget.

  2. Press the Zoom In button.

    Note: Only reporting widgets are capable of zooming. Non-reporting widgets–such as the Activities Grid–do not have this functionality.

  3. A window showing an enlarged view of the widget is displayed.

Changing the Date Range

You can adjust the date range used to populate a widget in one of two ways:

  1. Select a predefined date range from the Workspace menu bar. This applies the date range to every widget on the Workspace.

  2. Zoom in on a widget and select a predefined date range in the top-left corner of the window, or user the date range slider to fine tune the widget's date range.

    Note: The date range you select in this window only takes affect while this window is open. It does not modify the global date range for the workspace nor does it change the widget's date range in the workspace.
