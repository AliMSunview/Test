Removing Approvers

Occasionally, you may need to override ChangeGear by deleting approvers from a ticket.

To delete an approver:

  1. Navigate to the ticket module from the primary ChangeGear Navigation menu.

  2. Open the ticket you want to edit.

  3. Click the Approval tab.

  4. Select the approver you want to delete, and click Remove, (just above the approver list). A confirmation dialog box displays.

  5. Click Yes.

  6. On the ticket toolbar, click Save. The Saved dialog box displays.

  7. In the Comments box, type Deleted approver: (approver’s name).

  8. Click OK, and click Close.
