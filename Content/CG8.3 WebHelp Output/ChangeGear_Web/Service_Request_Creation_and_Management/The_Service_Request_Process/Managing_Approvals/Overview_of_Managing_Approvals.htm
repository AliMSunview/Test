Overview of Managing Approvals

The Approval tab supports the ticket life cycle by listing approvers and their decisions. Here, you can add, update, group, and delete approvers. You can also review the Approval tab to see who participates in the approval of the ticket and the status of their decisions.

Your local ChangeGear Administrator can add conditions and filtering criteria based on service request fields to add automatically an approval group, (e.g., Critical service requests).
