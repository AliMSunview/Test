Pending Close Service Requests

After you have resolved a service request, you generally wait for the original requester to validate the service fulfillment before closing the ticket. This situation allows the original requester to confirm if the service provided actually fulfilled the request. If the service request is not fulfilled, ChangeGear moves the service request back to the Fulfillment state.

Tip: Business Policy Automation rules can be configured to automatically close service requests after a certain amount of time with no response from the original requester.

To close a service request:

  1. Open the service request.

  2. In the toolbar, click Close.

  3. Choose the appropriate Close Type.

  4. In the Comment box, type an explanation for closing the service request, click Close.

  5. Click OK.
