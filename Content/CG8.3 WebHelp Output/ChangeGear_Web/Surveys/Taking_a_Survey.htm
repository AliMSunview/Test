Taking a Survey

Survey links are e-mailed to users either through a BPA rules or manually. Users can take the survey at any time, though they must be logged-in to ChangeGear to properly record the results. Survey results are only recorded after the Submit button has been pressed. If users are interrupted while taking a survey, they can retake it. However, surveys can only be submitted once per ticket. For example, a customer satisfaction survey can be taken multiple times if it is in reference to different tickets each time.
