Overview of the Survey Module

The ChangeGear Survey module allows IT managers to improve customer satisfaction by making it easy to create and publish surveys and then analyze the responses using the included survey reports. The reports summarize the responses, empowering your organization to clearly understand the feedback, adjust accordingly, and thereby improve customer satisfaction.

ChangeGear’s Survey module employs a streamlined creation process using both a Survey Editor and a Question Editor that can tightly integrate with Business Policy Automations (BPAs) to automatically send surveys to individuals or groups. Surveys can be automated by ticket type and by frequency of ticket submission, (i.e., once per 20 ticket submissions).

Two different types of surveys can be sent to users. Ticket Surveys are most common and are associated with a specific ticket ID. These are generally sent using BPAs, though you can manually send Ticket Surveys to users or groups. Stand-alone Surveys are sent without referencing a ticket. For example, if you want to send out a customer satisfaction survey for your users regardless of whether or not they have entered a ticket into ChangeGear, you would send a Stand-alone Survey.

You can view responses either from the Survey module or the Reports module. The Survey module allows you to see individual responses for each survey, while the Reports module allows you to view either individual responses or all the responses for a survey. Reports can be easily exported to Excel to further manipulate the data.
