Manually Sending a Survey

You can manually send a survey to a recipient. It can be either a Ticket Survey or Stand-alone Survey.

To send a survey:

  1. Open the Survey module from the ChangeGear primary navigation menu.

  2. Choose an appropriate view to select the survey.

  3. Open the survey you wish to send.

  4. Select Copy Link… The Survey Link dialog displays.

  5. If the survey relates to a ticket, choose the Module and Ticket ID that the survey references. You can leave these blank if you are sending a Stand-alone Survey.

  6. Click Apply to add the information to the link.

  7. Copy the link and send it to the recipient.
