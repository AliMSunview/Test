Deleting Surveys

Try to refrain from deleting surveys as this negates electronic tracking. Instead, retire surveys. Deleting a survey is a permanent change and cannot be undone. Results/responses of deleted surveys are no longer available.

To delete a survey:

  1. Open the Survey module from the ChangeGear primary navigation menu.

  2. Select the appropriate view.

  3. Select the survey you wish to delete.

  4. Click Delete from the toolbar.
