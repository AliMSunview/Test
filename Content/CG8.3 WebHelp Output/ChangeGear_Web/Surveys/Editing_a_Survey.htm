Editing a Survey

You can edit a survey at anytime during its life cycle. To view the status of the survey displays in the title bar of the Survey Editor. You can also navigate to the All Surveys view to see the status.

Note: If the survey is already published, you must click Revise before editing the survey.

To edit a survey:

  1. Open the Survey module from the ChangeGear primary navigation menu.

  2. Choose an appropriate view to select the survey.

  3. Open the survey. If the survey has already been published, click Revise.

  4. Edit the Survey text,

    -OR-

    Select a question and click Edit on the Question toolbar.

  5. Click Save when you have finished editing the survey.

Note: Editing a survey automatically sends the survey back to the draft state. It is necessary to republish the survey in order to use it.
