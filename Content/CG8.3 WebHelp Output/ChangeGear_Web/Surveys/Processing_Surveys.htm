Processing Surveys

When you view the Survey module, you are taken directly to the Draft view. The Draft view includes all the surveys that have not been published or retired.

Note: Should you not be able to access or view the module, please check your privileges in the Administration module or call your Serviceaide Software representative to ensure that you have the correct license.

  • The Published view only includes those surveys that have been published.
  • The Retired view shows surveys that have been retired from the published state.
  • The All Surveys view shows all surveys that have not been deleted from the system.

ChangeGear’s standard toolbar options are available; print, create, edit, duplicate, and delete a survey. You can also e-mail a link to a survey and refresh/navigate the grid.

Note: Should you wish to duplicate a survey, the copy will include the questions and settings, but does not affect the Business Policy Automations (BPA) settings of the original survey. A new BPA will have to be created to send out the duplicated survey, see the ChangeGear Administrator's Guide.
