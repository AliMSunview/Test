Retiring a Survey

When a survey has served its purpose, you can retire the survey. A retired survey is no longer available to be sent to customers, but the results are still available.

Note: If you decide that the survey was retired in error, you can simply publish the survey again from the Retired view.

To retire a survey:

  1. Open the Survey module from the ChangeGear primary navigation menu.

  2. Choose the Published view.

  3. Select the survey you wish to retire and double click or select Edit.

  4. In the Survey toolbar, select Retire.

  5. The survey is now retired and only visible in either the Retired view or the All Surveys view.
