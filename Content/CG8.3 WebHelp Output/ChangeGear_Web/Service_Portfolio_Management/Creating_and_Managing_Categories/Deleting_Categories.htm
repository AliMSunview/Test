Deleting Categories

Categories can be deleted from ChangeGear, though some care should be taken if the category has service items associated with it.

Note: You cannot delete a category with service items associated to it. If you must delete a category, move the service items to another category.

To delete a category:

  1. Edit relevant service items such that the items are no longer associated with the category.

  2. Select the category from the navigation menu.

  3. Click Delete.

  4. Confirm your choice in the dialog.
