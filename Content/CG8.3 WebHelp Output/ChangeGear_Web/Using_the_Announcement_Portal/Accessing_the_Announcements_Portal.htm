Accessing the Announcements Portal

While most service announcements are sent via e-mail, some are also published to ChangeGear's Web Announcement Portal.

Note: Announcements must be published to the ChangeGear Web Announcement Portal for users to view announcements on the Web.

Stakeholders

End users without access to ChangeGear can also view posted service announcements using this address in a browser:

http://<ChangeGearServer>/CGWeb/Announce/ViewAnnounceSchedulerCalendar.aspx (Replace <ChangeGearServer> with the name of your ChangeGear Server.)

Staff Users

Staff users can view announcements by navigating to a module, (e.g., incident, problem, change module), from the primary ChangeGear navigation menu and selecting Announcement Portal from the Other category on the navigation menu.

You can also view announcements from the Announcements tab of a Change Request on the ChangeGear Web or the Desktop Client. Open a Change ticket and click on the Announcements tab to view and maintain the announcements associated with the Change Request.

Note: You must have announcement privileges to add, edit, delete, review, or view announcements. Please contact your ChangeGear administrator to have these privileges set.
