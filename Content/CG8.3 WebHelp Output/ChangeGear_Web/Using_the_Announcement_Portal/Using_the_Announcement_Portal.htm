Overview of the Announcement Portal

ChangeGear's Announcement Portal is designed to communicate upcoming changes to a wide audience, both inside and outside your organization. It provides an effective notification system to all stakeholders in the change process throughout the change life cycle via e-mail and a web-based announcement portal.

Note: You may not have access to this ChangeGear feature depending on your permissions.

There are these types of users who use the Announcement Portal: stakeholders, staff, and administrators. Stakeholders view the Announcement Portal for announcements that may impact them. Staff create and process announcements, while administrators manage ChangeGear.
