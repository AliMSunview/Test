Overview of Managing Attachments

ChangeGear lets you attach support documents to your tickets. A ticket that includes attachments provides more information than a simple request. The ticket becomes the central repository for information regarding the nature of the ticket and the possible planning, testing, and training instructions around the ticket.

By managing attachments, you’re building efficiency into your service management system. Within each request, you can provide all of the support documents required for the request. Instead of searching through directories, your staff and others can find support documents directly in the ticket.

The Attachments tab lets you add, remove, and review files. You can make the following adjustments to the columns in the attachments list:

  • Move the columns

  • Adjust the column widths

  • Filter the columns

  • Sort the columns
