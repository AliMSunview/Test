Reviewing Attachments

To review an attachment:

  1. In the form, click the Attachments tab.

  2. In the Attachments list, double-click on the attachment you want to review

    - OR -

    Click on the attachment to select and click View in the Attachment toolbar.

  3. Confirm ChangeGear opens the application associated with the file.
