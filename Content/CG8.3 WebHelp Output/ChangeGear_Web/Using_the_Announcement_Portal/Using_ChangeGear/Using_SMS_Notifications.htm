Using SMS Notifications

If your ChangeGear administrator has enabled Short Message Service (SMS) support, you can receive notifications from ChangeGear directly on your mobile device. ChangeGear uses the phone number entered in the Mobile field of your user profile to send notifications. If you do not have a mobile phone number set, ChangeGear will prompt you to enter your mobile number when you access the Self-Service Portal. You can choose to dismiss this message you do not wish to provide your mobile number.

To enable SMS notifications:

  1. Log in to the ChangeGear Web client.

  2. Open your User Profile by clicking on your account name in the top-right corner of the screen.

  3. Enter your mobile device number in the Mobile text box.

    IMPORTANT: The format required may vary depending on your ChangeGear configuration. Please confirm the correct formatting with your ChangeGear administrator.

  4. Click the Receive Text Messages check box.

  5. Click the Save button to save your changes.
