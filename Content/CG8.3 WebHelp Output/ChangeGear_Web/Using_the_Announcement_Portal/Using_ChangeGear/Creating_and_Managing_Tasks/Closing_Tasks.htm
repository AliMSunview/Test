Closing Tasks

When you close a task, you should complete the fields that help determine the total cost of the task. The fields display the date and time you finished the task and its cost in time and effort. You can close a task both through the ticket form and through the Task Manager.

Tip: In ChangeGear, one person hour equals the duration in hours for one person to implement the task.

To close a task:

  1. Open the ticket.

    -OR-

    Open the Task Manager and open the task go to step 3.

  2. In the tab section, (Implementation tab for RFC or Tasks tab for other tickets), double-click the task.

  3. In the Completion Date field, enter the date and time that you completed the task.

  4. In the Duration field, enter the total number hours required for the implementation task.

  5. In the Effort field, enter the total number of person hours required for the implementation task.

  6. In the Task toolbar, select Close Task.
