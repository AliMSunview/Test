Retracting and Pulling Back Announcements

Retract

You can retract an announcement after it has been published. Sent e-mails cannot be retracted, but announcements published to the Announcement Portal are removed. The status of the announcement is automatically set to reviewed after the announcement is retracted.

To retract an announcement:

  1. Open a published announcement.

  2. Click Retract.

Pull back

You can pull back an announcement after it has been reviewed and before it has been published. The status of the announcement is automatically set to unreviewed after the announcement has been pulled back.

To pullback an announcement:

  1. Open a reviewed announcement.

  2. Click Pullback.
