Publishing Announcements

After an announcement has been reviewed, you can publish the announcement. If your organization has a review process, a notification e-mail may be sent to the creator stating that the announcement has been approved and can now be published. The creator then publishes the announcement.

To publish an announcement:

  1. Open the received e-mail and click on the link to open the Announcement Details dialog.

    -OR-

    Go directly to the announcement in ChangeGear.

  2. Reconfirm the recipients and whether or not it will be posted to the Announcement Portal.

  3. Click Publish. A confirmation dialog appears.

  4. Click OK.
