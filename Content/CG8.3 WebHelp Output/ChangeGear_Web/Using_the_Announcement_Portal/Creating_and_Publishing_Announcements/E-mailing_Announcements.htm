E-mailing Announcements

You can e-mail individuals announcements, even individuals who are outside of your organization. For example, if you need to notify a vendor of a change that affects them, you can e-mail the vendor the announcement.

To e-mail announcements when they are published:

  1. Use the tabs to navigate which type of stakeholder you'd like to e-mail:

    • Users—Select individual users.

    • Teams—Select teams.

    • Roles—Select roles.

    • Dynamic Roles—Select dynamically generated roles.

    • Contacts—Select external stakeholders. Contacts are maintained by the ChangeGear Administrators.

  2. Select a user, team, contact, or role and then click Add. The selected person, team, or role will appear in the Selected Items section.

  3. If you accidentally add a person, team, or role, simply select the item in the Selected Items section and click Remove.

  4. Click OK. The To... field should now reflect your selections.

If you wish to e-mail the announcement to someone not in your organization and not in the Contacts tab, contact your ChangeGear Administrator to have the contact added. You can also use the Other Recipients field in the Details tab.

To e-mail announcements after they are published:

  1. Open the Announcement Portal (see Accessing the Announcements Portal).

    - OR -

    Open the Announcement Manager.

  2. Open the Announcement you wish to e-mail.

  3. Click the Print icon. The print view displays.

  4. Click the E-mail icon. Your default e-mail application opens with a link to the announcement.

  5. Enter your recipient's e-mail address and send the e-mail.
