Using the Detail Tab

The Detail tab lets you modify or add information to your announcement. The announcement appears on the Scheduled tab or Unscheduled tab in the Announcement Portal based on the details provided on this dialog.

Other fields you can enter are:

  • Downtime Required: Lets the users know when equipment or services are unavailable.

  • Location: The location can be used on the Announcement Portal to filter announcements by a specific location on the calendar.

  • Group: Identify the group responsible for this announcement.

  • Start and End Date/Time: Describes the time frame and duration for the announced activity.

  • Type: The announcement appears on the Scheduled tab or Unscheduled tab in the Announcement Portal when the announcement is published.

  • Impacted Services: You may add free form text for any services affected by the announcement activity.

  • Other Recipients: You can add e-mail addresses of stakeholders outside the organization in this field.

  • Publish when RFC is: You can select when you would like the announcement published based on the status of the Change request.
