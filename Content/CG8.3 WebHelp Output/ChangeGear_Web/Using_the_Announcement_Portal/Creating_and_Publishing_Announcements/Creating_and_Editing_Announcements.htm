Creating and Editing Announcements

You can create or edit announcements in the Change form under the Announcements tab. You can also view a history of announcements for the Change from this tab, as well as create, edit, and delete announcements from this tab.

Announcements created through the Announcements tab are tied to a specific Change Request. If you want to create an Announcement independent of a Change, see Creating Freestanding Announcements.

Note: You must have announcement privileges to add, edit, delete, or approve announcements. Please contact your ChangeGear administrator to have these privileges set.

Tip: After you have saved an announcement, you can preview what it will look like when it is published by selecting Preview Mode in the Announcement editor.

To create or edit an announcement:

  1. Open a Change request and click the Announcements tab.

  2. If you are creating an announcement, click New in the Announcement tool bar.

    - OR -

    If you are editing an announcement, select the announcement and click View in the announcement tool bar, skip to the General Tab.

  3. If you are creating a new announcement, choose Default or Template in the Create Announcement window.

    • Default automatically fills in the Subject and Body with the Change summary and description.

    • Template fills in the Subject and Body using Communication Templates, after which you can edit the announcement.

  4. Click OK.

General Tab

In the General tab in the Announcement dialog you can enter the basic announcement information.

  1. Click To... to select a user, team, role, dynamic role, or contact using the appropriate tab. Press the Add button to add the selection to the To... list, you can also remove selections from the To... list. When list is complete, scroll to the bottom of the dialog and press OK.

    Tip: You can just publish the announcement to the Announcements Portal by leaving the To... field blank. Click for more information.

  2. Edit the Subject field by clicking in the field and typing your new information.

  3. In the Body field, type your announcement information and use the tool bar to format your message.

  4. If it is necessary to review the announcement, select a Reviewer from the reviewer list or press ... to select from a list of users.

    Note: If your company policy allows it, ChangeGear can be configured to skip the review process.

  5. If you do not want the announcement posted on the announcement portal, uncheck Post on Web Announcement Portal (when Published).

  6. If the announcement is urgent, check Urgent Announcement.

Detail Tab

Use the Detail tab to add announcement details such as activity date and time information, a specific e-mail address, schedule activity, and other information. For more information, see Using the Details Tab.

  • To send the announcement to the reviewer you selected, click Request Review.

  • To complete the announcement process, click Publish.

Note: You can confirm the status of the announcement at the top of the dialog window, (e.g., draft).

Tip: Instead of sending all of the announcement details in an e-mail to your users, you can send a link to the Announcements Portal. Users are not required to have ChangeGear in order to view the Announcement Portal.
