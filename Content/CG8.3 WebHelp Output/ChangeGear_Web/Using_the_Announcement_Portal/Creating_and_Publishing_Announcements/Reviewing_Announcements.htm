Reviewing Announcements

If your organization has a review process, announcements must be reviewed prior to publication. If you have Announcement Approval privileges, you can designate yourself as a Reviewer and publish your announcements as needed. For smaller companies, we recommend designating Implementers who can publish announcements without a review.

Reviewers will receive an e-mail requesting announcement approvals.

Note: You must have announcement approval privileges to review and approve announcements.

To review an announcement:

  1. Open the e-mail you received requesting your review of an announcement.

  2. Click on the link in the e-mail to open the announcement.

  3. Review the announcement.

  4. Click either Approve or Revise. Revise sends the announcement back to its creator for revision.

  5. Provide feedback in the Announcement Feedback dialog.

  6. The creator can either publish the announcement or further revise it depending on whether the reviewer approved the announcement or sent it back for revision.
