Service Announcements Details

The Announcements tab provides details about announcements related to the ticket.

  • ID - the unique ID of the announcement.

  • Status - the status of the announcement (reviewed, unreviewed, or published).

  • Summary - a brief summary of the impact of the announcement.

  • Reviewer - the reviewer assigned to the announcement.

  • Announced by - the person or role who created and published the announcement.
