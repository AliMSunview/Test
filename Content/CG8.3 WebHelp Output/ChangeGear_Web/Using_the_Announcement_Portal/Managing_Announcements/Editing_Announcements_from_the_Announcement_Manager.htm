Editing Announcements from the Announcement Manager

You can edit announcements at any time during the announcement life-cycle.

Note: You can confirm the status of the announcement at the top of the dialog window, (e.g., draft).

To modify an announcement:

  1. Open the Announcement Manager from a ChangeGear module under the Other menu.

  2. Select the Announcement you want to change, and click Edit. The Announcement Details dialog box displays.

  3. In the General tab, you can change or add recipients, edit the subject or body of the message, or change reviewers. You can refine your announcement further using the Detail tab.

  4. Choose an appropriate action or click OK.
