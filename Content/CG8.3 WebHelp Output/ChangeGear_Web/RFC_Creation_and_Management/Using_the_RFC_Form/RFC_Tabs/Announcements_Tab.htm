Announcements Tab

The Announcements tab lets you create and edit announcements that are tied to a specific Change Request. You can view, modify, and delete a history of announcements for the request. If you want to create an Announcement independent of a change request, see Creating Freestanding Announcements.

Note: You must have announcement privileges to add, edit, delete, or approve announcements. Please contact your ChangeGear administrator to have these privileges set.

Tip: After you have saved an announcement, you can preview what it will look like when it is published by selecting Preview Mode in the Announcement editor.

To create an announcement:

  1. Open a change request and click the Announcements tab.

  2. Click New in the Announcement tool bar.

  3. Choose the E-mail template that will be used as the basis for the announcement e-mail.

    • Default automatically fills in the Subject and Body with the Change summary and description.

    • E-mail Template fills in the Subject and Body using a Communication Template. For more information on Communication Templates, please contact your ChangeGear administrator.

  4. Click OK.

  5. Click To... to select a user, team, role, dynamic role, or contact using the appropriate tab. Press the Add button to add the selection to the To... list, you can also remove selections from the To... list. When list is complete, scroll to the bottom of the dialog and press OK.

    Tip: You can just publish the announcement to the Announcements Portal by leaving the To... field blank. Click for more information.

  6. Edit the Subject field by clicking in the field and typing your new information.

  7. In the Body field, type your announcement information and use the tool bar to format your message.

  8. If it is necessary to review the announcement, select a Reviewer from the reviewer list or press ... to select from a list of users.

    Note: If your company policy allows it, ChangeGear can be configured to skip the review process.

  9. If you do not want the announcement posted on the announcement portal, uncheck Post on Web Announcement Portal (when Published).

  10. If the announcement is urgent, check Urgent Announcement.

To edit an announcement:

  1. Open a change request and click the Announcements tab.

  2. Select the announcement and click View in the announcement tool bar.

  3. Add your changes to the announcement.

  4. Click Save to save your changes.

For more information about creating announcements see Creating and Editing Announcements.
