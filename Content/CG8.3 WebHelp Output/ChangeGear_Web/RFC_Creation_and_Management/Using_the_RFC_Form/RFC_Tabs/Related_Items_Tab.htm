Related Items Tab

You can relate different items to tickets using the Related Items tab on the RFC form. You can relate incidents, problems, RFCs, and Knowledge Base articles. Correctly linking to other requests or articles is important to reporting and tracking. For example, a bug may have a higher priority if it has many associated incidents. Linking requests together is also useful in reporting the worth of a system—if it's down for X minutes, how many incidents were logged against it.

To link to a related item:

  1. Open an RFC.

  2. Open the Related Items tab.

  3. Click the Link button in the Related Items toolbar. The Link Item dialogLink Item dialog appears.

  4. Click on the tab with the type of item you want to relate the item to.

  5. Select an item and click Add. You can relate the RFC to more than one item at a time.

  6. After you are done adding items, click OK.

  7. Your RFC will now have related items in the tab.

To unlink a related item:

  1. Open an RFC.

  2. Open Related Items tab.

  3. Select the RFC you want to unlink.

  4. Click the Unlink button in the Related Items toolbar.
