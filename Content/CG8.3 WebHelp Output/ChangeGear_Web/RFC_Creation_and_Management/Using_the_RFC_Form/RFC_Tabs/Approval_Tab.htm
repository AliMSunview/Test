Approval Tab

Approval is a formal decision to implement or reject a change request. Information previously collected for the change request plays a vital role during this phase of the change management life cycle. For example, approval depends on the benefits identified during the submission of the change request. Approval also depends on the risk, impact, and cost identified during analysis.

To approve an RFC:

  1. Click to open the change request requiring your approval.

  2. Click the Approve button. (If you are not an approver, an error message appears).

  3. Enter any comments you may have in the Comments field.

  4. Click OK.

Note: If you are the last to approve an RFC, its status is automatically change from Pending Approval to Assigned.

While the ChangeGear administrator will create approval groups, you can also manage the approvers for a particular ticket if you have the Edit Approvers privilege.

To add Approvers:

  1. Open the Approval tab in the RFC.

  2. Click Add.

  3. Select a user and click Add.

  4. Click OK to add the user as an Approver.

  5. Continue selecting and adding approvers until you've added all the approvers you want.

To remove Approvers:

  1. Select the approver you want to remove.

  2. Click Remove.

To group or ungroup Approvers:

  1. Select the approvers you want to group together.

    • Hold the SHIFT key when you click to select multiple approvers that are next to each other.

    • Hold the CTRL key when you click to select multiple approvers that are not next to each other.

  2. Click the Group button.

  3. Select an approver and click Ungroup to remove the approver from the group.

To reorder Approvers:

  1. Select the approver.

  2. Click the Move Up or Move Down to reorder the approvers.
