Overview of Planning Time and Effort

No project plan is complete without an estimate of the time and effort needed to complete the change request. ChangeGear lets you plan the time and effort of an RFC on Analysis tab of the RFC form. You can estimate start and completion dates. You can also estimate the duration of the project in hours and the effort in person hours.
