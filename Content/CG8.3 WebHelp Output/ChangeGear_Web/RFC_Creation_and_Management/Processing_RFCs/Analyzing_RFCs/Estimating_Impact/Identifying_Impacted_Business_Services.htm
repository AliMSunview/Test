Identifying Impacted Business Services

When you review a ticket, you may find that the requester did not specify the business service(s) impacted. You can review the Impacted Business Services field in the form via a drop-down menu or the Impact Analysis dialog. If you know the name of the impacted business service, it is recommended to use the menu to select it. Otherwise, open the Impact Analysis dialog.

Note: Some business services will automatically be entered if they are related to a selected impacted resource. As such, those business services cannot be removed from the ticket.

To identify the impacted business services via the drop-down menu:

  1. Open the ticket.

  2. Click the downward-facing arrow by Impacted Business Services. The Impacted Business Services menu displays.

  3. Select business services from the menu.

    Tip: You can use the type-ahead feature to narrow your choices.

  4. Click the button to add the service to the list of selected business services.

  5. Save the ticket.

    Note: You can remove business services from the Selected Impacted Business Services list by using the button. You can also view the details of a business service by using the button.

To identify the impacted business services via the Impact Analysis dialog:

  1. Open the ticket.

  2. Click the button by Impacted Business Services. The Impact Analysis dialog displays.

    Note: Depending on the module, you may not be able to view all sections of the Impact Analysis dialog.

  3. Click Add by the Business Services field. The Select Items dialog displays.

  4. Click Add by the business service to add it to the Selected Item box.

  5. If you have selected a service in error, you may remove an item from the box by selecting it on the Selected Items grid and clicking Remove.

  6. Click OK. The service has now been added to the Business Services field.

  7. Click OK to save changes made to the Impact Analysis dialog.

  8. Save the ticket.

Note: You can remove services from the Business Services list by clicking Remove. You can also view the details of a selected service by clicking Properties.
