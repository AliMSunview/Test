Overview of Estimating Impact

Every RFC carries a potential impact on resources and users. To analyze an RFC thoroughly, you must determine the extent of the RFC impacts. ChangeGear provides a consistent method for estimating the impact of your RFCs.

With ChangeGear’s tools, you can provide a detailed and precise estimate of the RFC’s impact.
