Estimating Impact

Every RFC represents a potential impact for resources, business services, and users and must be analyzed to either lessen the potential impact or identify the potential impact of changes.

To estimate the impact of an RFC:

  1. In the Change tab, open the RFC.

  2. In the data fields section, determine if the Impacted Resources field displays the resources impacted by the RFC. If the Impacted Resources box does not display the resources impacted, see Identifying Impacted Resources.

  3. In the data fields section, determine if the Impacted Business Services field displays the business services impacted by the RFC. If the Impacted Business Services box does not display the resources impacted, see Identifying Impacted Business Services.

  4. In the data fields section, determine if the Impacted Users field displays the users impacted by the RFC. If the Impacted Users/Groups box does not display the users impacted, see Identifying Impacted Users.

  5. In the Risk field, select the level of risk carried by the RFC.

  6. In the Analysis tab, enter the probable consequences if the change is not implemented into the If Not Implemented field.

  7. In the Analysis tab, type a description of any alternative solutions to the change request in the Alternative Solution(s) field.

  8. In the Analysis tab, type a description of any side effects that the RFC may produce in Possible Side Effects field.

Important: The requester may have already provided alternative solutions. Do not overwrite existing alternatives. Type your solution above or below the existing alternative and annotate it.
