Escalating RFCs

You can escalate an RFC to obtain authorization from another user before proceeding. When you escalate an RFC, ChangeGear automatically activates a predefined notification rule that sends a template-based e-mail to the appropriate user(s).

To escalate an RFC:

  1. Click the Change tab to open the corresponding module.

  2. Select the appropriate RFC. The RFC status must be in the New or More Info status, (i.e., the ticket has been submitted and is in New state -or- you have selected More Info from the More Actions menu in the toolbar).

  3. In the Assigned To field, select the person to whom you are escalating the RFC.

    Tip: If you're escalating to a person who isn't a ChangeGear user, leave the RFC assigned to yourself and e-mail it to that individual.

  4. In the RFC toolbar, click Escalate, (the option may appear in the More Actions menu depending on ticket stage).

  5. In the Comment box, type any comments you have.

  6. Click OK to save.
