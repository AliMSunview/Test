Rejecting RFCs

If you determine that you cannot justify the risk or impact of a ticket, you can reject it. When you reject a ticket, ChangeGear automatically sends an e-mail notification to the requester.

To reject a ticket:

  1. Open the appropriate ticket.

  2. Click Reject.

  3. In the Comment box, type an explanation for the rejection.

  4. In the Close Type list, select Rejected.

  5. Click OK to save.
