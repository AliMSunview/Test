Adding Comments

The best way to describe any changes you have made to an RFC is to record your comments when you save the changes. You may, however, decide to record changes to an RFC after you close the form. ChangeGear lets you add history records to RFC forms after the fact. The way to recognize a manually added history record is to look for the value Comment in the Action field of the history list.

To add a history comment:

  1. Open the RFC.

  2. Click the History tab.

  3. On the History toolbar click Add.

  4. In the History Records Details dialog box, type your comments.

  5. If you clicked Add from the History tab, you will need to click Save on the RFC.

  6. Click OK.
