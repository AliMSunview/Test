Reviewing the History List

The first level of reviewing an ticket history is to view all of the history records together. From this point of view, you gain a broad perspective of the tickets history. More specifically, you can gather the following information about each historical item:

  • Who changed the ticket (Name field)

  • When the change occurred (Created field)

  • What kind of change occurred (Action field)

  • Any comments the person recorded about the change (Comment field)

If the ticket has a long history list, you can sort its history records as necessary. By sorting, you can reorder and reorganize the history list to show only the records you want to view.

To review the history list:

  1. Open the RFC record you want to review or audit.

  2. In the RFC form, click the History tab.

  3. Sort the primary column in ascending or descending order as necessary.

  4. When you finish reviewing the history list, click Close.
