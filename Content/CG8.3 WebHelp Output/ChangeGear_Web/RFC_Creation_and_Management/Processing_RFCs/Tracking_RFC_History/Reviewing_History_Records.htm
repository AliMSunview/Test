Reviewing History Records

Each history record displays the date it was created and the name of the creator. You can either review the history records within the list or view them separately. The creator of the history record may also add comments to explain changes made to the RFC.

To View an RFC History Record:

  1. In the grid, double-click the RFC record that you want to review.

  2. In the RFC form, click the History tab.

  3. In the History list, double-click the history record that you want to review.

    - OR -

    Select the history record you want to review, and click the View button in the History toolbar.

  4. In the View History Details dialog box, review any recorded comments.

  5. Click Close.
