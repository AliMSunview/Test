Commenting On Your Tests

If you handle a minor RFC that requires testing, you can document it with a few comments. And if you’re handling a significant or major RFC, you can add comments to supplement your testing attachments.

Your comments can enhance or explain anything not covered by your testing documents. You can use the Comment boxes, in the Quality Assurance and deployment group, to document your tests in the absence of existing documentation.

Note: If you add comments to the QA or Deployment group, be careful not to overwrite existing comments.

To comment on your tests:

  1. On the RFC form, click the Release tab.

  2. Determine whether you’re commenting on a test that occurred before or after the implementation of the change request.

    • If the test occurred before implementation, type your comments in the Quality Assurance section of the Release tab.

    • If the test occurred after implementation, type your comments in the Deployment section of the Release tab.

  3. Save your changes to the RFC form.
