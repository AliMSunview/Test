The Deployment State

The changes are deployed into the production environment. Appropriate staff and stakeholders are notified of deployment activities and results. Related tasks, such as training and documentation delivery are completed. Post‐deployment lessons learned information is collected by release manager to document the process for future reference. During this stage, implementers continue to update and close tasks as appropriate.

To advance to the Deployment stage:

  1. Open the RFC.

  2. In the toolbar, click Approve for Deployment.

Ensuring Related Processes are in Place

The release manager coordinates with appropriate staff that related processes, such as training and documentation delivery, are performed.

Documenting Time, Effort, and Lessons Learned

The release manager verifies that the CMDB has been updated with the most current information. Actual time and effort required for release management tasks are updated to reflect results and for the change manager to gauge the success of the release process.
