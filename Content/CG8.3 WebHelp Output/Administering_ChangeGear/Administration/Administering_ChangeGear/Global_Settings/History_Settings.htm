History Settings

The Repository Maintenance section lets you configure how long certain history items are kept. You can control the amount of time that security, alert, and scan histories are kept. Items older than the number of months you have selected are removed from the history file, saving space on your server and improving system performance.

  • Security History - Records user log in/out activity

  • Alert History - Records when business policy automations are sent

  • Scan History - Records when an item was scanned and what was found

To change the history settings:

  1. Click the Administration tab.

  2. In the Administration section, click Global Settings.

  3. Scroll down to the Repository Maintenance section.

  4. Select which history file to modify.

  5. Use the up and down arrows to change how long historic records are kept.

    Note: Reducing the time history records are kept can increase your ChangeGear server's performance.

  6. Click Save to keep your configuration changes.
