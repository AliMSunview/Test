Knowledge Base Settings

The Knowledge Base section lets you configure how knowledge base (KB) articles are managed by users.

Users have the ability to rate KB articles based on their perceived helpfulness. Ratings are stored on a five-point scale, where one point means the article was not helpful and five points means the article was very helpful. Users can rate each article one time as long as the article has been published.

By default, ChangeGear represents ratings using stars. ChangeGear also provides a thumbs up/thumbs down-style rating system, where a thumbs up is the equivalent of a five-point rating and a thumbs down is the equivalent of a one-point rating. The Ratings option lets you toggle between these two rating methods.

To switch to a thumbs-based rating system:

  1. Click the Administration tab.

  2. In the Administration section, click Global Settings.

  3. Scroll down to the Knowledge Base Settings section.

  4. Use the radio buttons to choose between the 5-Star Rating System or the "Thumbs Up/Down" Rating System.

  5. Click Save.
