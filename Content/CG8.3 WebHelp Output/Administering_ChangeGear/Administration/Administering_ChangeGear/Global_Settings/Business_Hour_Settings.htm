Business Hour Settings

Business Hours let you specify your organization's operating hours in order to provide more accurate reporting on time calculations, such as the amount of time spent working on tickets. Setting business hours ensures that when time is reported, only the hours that a technician was available to work on an item will count towards the response time. For example, if your technicians only process requests from 9:00 AM – 5:00 PM, a ticket that is submitted at 4:45 PM and resolved at 9:15 AM the next day will show a 30 minute response time.

Business hours are particularly useful when combined with Service Level Agreements (SLAs), as they provide more accurate tracking of ticket resolution times.

Note: This section explains how to configure business hours that apply to all tickets in ChangeGear. For more information on configuring SLA-specific business hours, see Configuring Operating Calendars.

To define global business hours:

  1. Click the Administration tab.

  2. In the Administration section, click Global Settings.

  3. Scroll down to the Business Hours section.

  4. Select the business days that the calendar will follow by using the check boxes. The default is Monday through Friday.

  5. Set the daily Start and End times by using the up and down arrows, or by entering the time in the text boxes.

    Note: Although you can set the time using 1-minute increments, ChangeGear will round the time up to the nearest quarter hour (or 15-minute increment).

  6. Click Save to keep your configuration changes.

Defining Global Holidays

You can also factor holidays into your business hours. A holiday is a 24-hour period where no time tracking is performed. SLAs will include holidays when calculating ticket resolution times.

To add a global holiday:

  1. Click the drop-down under Select Holidays to open the holiday calendar.

  2. Select a date and click Add.

    Important: Holidays are not recurring. ChangeGear will only treat the day specified as a holiday.

  3. To remove a holiday, select a date in the list of dates and click Remove.

    Note: You may enter up to sixty-four (64) holidays per year up to two years in advance.

  4. Click Save to keep your configuration changes.
