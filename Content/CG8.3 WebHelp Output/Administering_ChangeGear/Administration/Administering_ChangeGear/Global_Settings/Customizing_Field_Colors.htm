Customizing Field Colors

The General Settings section lets you select the color of required fields in the forms.

To change the color of required fields:

  1. Click the Administration tab.

  2. In the Administration section, click Global Settings.

  3. Scroll down to the General Settings section.

  4. Click the color icon to open the Color dialog.

  5. Select a color from the Color dialog or define a custom color and click OK.

    NOTE: You will need to recycle the CGWeb application pool (or simply restart IIS) to see your changes.

  6. Click Save to keep your configuration changes.
