Scheduling Surveys

Surveys can be scheduled using Business Policy Automations (BPAs) to be sent automatically to customers. The BPA will determine who will receive surveys and how frequently they will be received.

Note: The instructions will follow a scenario to send a survey to an incident requester every tenth time an incident request is closed.

To schedule ticket surveys:

  1. In the Administration module, navigate to Incident Management > Business Policy Automation.

    Note: If you are scheduling a different type of ticket survey, go to that management node and select Business Policy Automation, i.e., if you were scheduling a survey upon completion of a change request, you would go to Change Management > Business Policy Automation.

  2. In the Common Tasks menu, click New. The Business Rule DetailsBusiness Rule Details dialog box is displayed.

  3. In the Name field, type a name for the Business Rule, such as "Service Desk Satisfaction Survey."

  4. In the Description field, type a brief description of the Business Rule.

    Note: Ensure that the Enable check box is selected if you want the BPA to run.

  5. Specify how often you want ChangeGear to check for conditions matching your BPA.

  6. In the MaxOccurs box, specify how many times you want the BPA to execute per item. For example, if the number of rules is set to one and an RFC matches the conditions of the rule 1000 times, the BPA will only be run once.

    Note: Click Execute anytime you want to run a rule instantly.

  7. Open the Conditions tabConditions tab.

  8. Use the lists to specify conditions that, when met, will trigger the BPA. In this case, choose Status equals Closed, Close Type equals Resolved - Confirmed, and Modified In the Past > 01.00:00 to select only those closed tickets which have been confirmed as resolved more than a day ago. For more information, see Specifying Business Rule Conditions.

  9. Open the Actions tabActions tab to specify the actions that execute when the Business Rule is triggered (for more information, see Specifying Business Rule Actions).

  10. Click New.

  11. Choose Send Survey. The Survey Details dialogSurvey Details dialog box displays.

  12. Enter the Name and Description of the action.

  13. Select a Survey from the drop down menu. In this example, we have chosen the "Service Desk Satisfaction Survey."

    Note: Only published surveys are displayed.

  14. Choose the Frequency to send the survey to recipients based on when the conditions are met. For example, if the condition were for completed incident reports, you would have to choose if you want a survey sent every time a user had an incident ticket closed or if you want a survey sent once out of twenty completed incident tickets. In the scenario of sending a survey every tenth entry, set the Frequency to 1 out of 10 times.

  15. Select Edit to edit the e-mail sent to the survey recipient. You can use e-mail templates to unify the look and feel of your communications.

  16. Click Add… to add the recipients of the survey. You can select individuals, roles, or dynamic roles. In this scenario, select the role of Requester.

  17. Ensure that the Enable box is checked.

  18. Click OK to finish setting up your action.

  19. Click OK to complete setting up your BPA.

    Note: If you wish to run the BPA immediately, click Execute on the General tab.
