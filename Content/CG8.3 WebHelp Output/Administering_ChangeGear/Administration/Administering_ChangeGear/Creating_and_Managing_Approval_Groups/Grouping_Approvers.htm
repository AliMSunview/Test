Grouping Approvers

By default, approvers receive ticket notification in sequence: Approver 1 receives notification of the ticket, and after he/she approves it, Approver 2 receives notification, and so on. However, you can group two or more approvers so the approvers receive notification at the same time (in parallel).

To group approvers:

  1. Click the Administration tab to open the corresponding module.

  2. Navigate to the Change or Service Request module, e.g.,Change Management > Approval Groups. The Approval Groups page displays.

  3. Select the Approval Group you want to edit, and click Edit in the Common Tasks menu. The Approval Group Details dialog box displays.

  4. Select the approvers you want to group together by holding down the Ctrl key and clicking each of the desired entries.

  5. Click Group. The approvers you grouped together have the same Order number; the approvers will receive notifications simultaneously.
