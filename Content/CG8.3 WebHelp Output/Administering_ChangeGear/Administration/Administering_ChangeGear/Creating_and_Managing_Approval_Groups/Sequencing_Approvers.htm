Sequencing Approvers

Approvers receive ticket notification in sequence - Approver 1 receives the ticket before Approver 2, and so on. You can modify the approver sequence to suit your needs.

To modify the approver sequence:

  1. Click Administration.

  2. In the Administration module, navigate to Change or Service Request module, e.g., Change Management > Approval Groups. The Approval Groups page displays.

  3. Select the Approval Group you want to edit, and click Edit in the Common Tasks menu. The Approval Group Details dialog box displays.

  4. Select the approver you want to move, and click Move Up or Move Down until it appears in the correct sequence.

  5. Repeat Step 4 as necessary for additional approvers.

  6. Click OK.
