Automating Announcements

If your edition of ChangeGear includes the Announcement Portal, you may wish to automate the creation and/or publication of announcements. The Announcement Portal uses the Business Policy Automation feature to communicate every change or specific categories of change automatically. Since announcements are automated using the Business Policy Automation feature, many variations exist.

The following are two common ways of automating announcements.

  • Automated creation with automated publication—no user interaction is required after the rule is setup. Each RFC that matches the Business Rule criteria is published automatically. The content of the Announcement is constructed from the RFC's content using fields in an e-mail template.

  • Manual creation with automated publication—for each RFC, a user manually creates one or more announcements and requests review of the announcement(s). After review, the Business Rule publishes the announcement according to the criteria specified in the rule, e.g., at a time relative to the planned start or complete date.
