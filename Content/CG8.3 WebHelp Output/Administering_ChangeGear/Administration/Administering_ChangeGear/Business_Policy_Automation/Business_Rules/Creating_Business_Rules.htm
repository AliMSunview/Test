Creating Business Rules

ChangeGear's Business Policy Automation feature lets administrators monitor data and trigger specific actions, or Business Rules. For example, using Business Rules, ChangeGear can be configured to send an e-mail notification before an ticket is due or a contract expires. Once past due, the ticket can be automatically escalated to management.

To create a Business Policy Automation:

  1. In the Administration tree, select Business Policy Automation under the management node you wish to modify.

  2. In the Common Tasks menu, click New. The Business Rule Details dialog box displays.

  3. In the Name field, type a name for the Business Rule.

  4. In the Description field, type a brief description of the Business Rule.

    Note: Ensure that the Enable this rule check box is selected. If you don't want the rule to be active, clear the check box.

  5. Specify how frequently you want ChangeGear to check for conditions matching your rule.

  6. In the Number of rules box, specify how many times you want the BPA Rule to execute per item. For example, if the Number of rules value is set to one, the BPA Rule action will only be run once.

  7. Optionally, click Execute if you want to immediately run the rule.

  8. Click the Conditions tab.

  9. Use the lists to specify condition(s) that, when met, will trigger the Business Rule (for more information, see Specifying Business Rule Conditions).

    Note: Available condition options depend on the Item Type selected.

  10. Click the Actions tab to specify the actions that execute when the Business Rule is triggered (for more information, see Specifying Business Rule Actions).

  11. Click OK.
