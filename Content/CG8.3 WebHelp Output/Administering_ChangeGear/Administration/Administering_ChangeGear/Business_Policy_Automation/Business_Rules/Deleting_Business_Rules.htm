Deleting Business Rules

If you don't plan to use a Business Rule in the future, you can delete it.

To delete a Business Rule:

  1. In the Administration tree, select Business Policy Automation under the management node you wish to modify.

  2. Click the Business Rule you want to delete, and then click Delete in the Common Tasks menu. A confirmation message displays.

  3. To permanently delete the rule, click Yes.
