Business Policy Automation

ChangeGear’s Business Policy Automation feature allows administrators to define business rules that trigger specific actions, such as:

  • Electronic assignment or escalation of requests

  • Automatic notification of important developments

  • Follow-up reminders

Business rules help ensure timely completion and communication of requested changes and relieve management and staff of many manual tasks. For example, using business rules, ChangeGear can be configured to send an e-mail notification before a ticket is due. Once past due, the ticket can be automatically escalated to management. Using business rules, ChangeGear can also be configured to send an e-mail notification before a contract expires.

Depending on your role in ChangeGear or the version of ChangeGear you purchased, you may not have access to the Business Policy Automations section.
