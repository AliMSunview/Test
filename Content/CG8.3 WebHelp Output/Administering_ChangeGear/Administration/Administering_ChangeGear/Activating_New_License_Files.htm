Activating New License Files

An Administrator can activate new licenses through the Help menu should your organization purchase additional DTAs, user seats, add-ons, or other editions.

Note: You can activate ChangeGear on a maximum of three unique machines. If you need to activate ChangeGear on more than three machines, you will need to e-mail activation@sunviewsoftware.com.

To activate a new license:

  1. Open the desktop client.

  2. In the Help menu, select Activate License...

  3. In the Activation Assistant, enter the activation key you received from Serviceaide Software.

  4. Click Next.

  5. After the license has finished activating, restart your ChangeGear desktop client.
