Managing Tasks

Several ticket modules allow users to record a history of activity on a ticket in the form of tasks. Tasks provide a detailed account of user activity on a ticket including the nature of the actions performed, the user who performed those actions, and the total cost of those actions in terms of time and effort. Tasks can also be modified and reassigned over the course of their life cycle.

In the ChangeGear Web client, tasks are treated as part of a ticket module. However, in the ChangeGear Desktop client, tasks are listed as separate modules with their own lookup lists, automations, views, templates, notification rules, and workflows.

Note: For more information on using tasks, please refer to the ChangeGear User's Guide.
