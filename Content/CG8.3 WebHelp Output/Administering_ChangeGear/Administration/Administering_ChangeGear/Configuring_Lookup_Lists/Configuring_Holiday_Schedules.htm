Configuring Holiday Schedules

Holiday Schedules are used to exclude certain days from SLA time tracking, essentially pausing the SLA clock for a pre-determined period of time. Unlike global holidays, Holiday Schedules are configured on a per-SLA basis.

Holiday Schedules are not applied directly to SLAs. Instead, you need to apply a Holiday Schedule to an Operating Calendar, then apply the Operating Calendar to the SLA. When the Operating Calendar is applied to the SLA, the Holiday Schedule overrides the Global Business Hours setting. For more information, see Configuring Operating Calendars.

To create a Holiday Schedule:

  1. Click the Administration tab.

  2. In the Administration section, click General.

  3. Click Holiday Schedules.

  4. Under Common Tasks, click New. The Holiday Schedule Details dialog appears.

  5. In the Name field, enter a name for the new schedule.

  6. In the Description field, enter a description of the new schedule.

  7. Click the drop-down next to Select Date to open the calendar.

  8. Select a date and click Add. You can only add dates that occur after today.

    Important: Holidays are not recurring. ChangeGear will only treat the specified date as a holiday.

  9. To remove a holiday, select a date in the list of dates and click Remove.

    Note: You may enter up to sixty-four (64) holidays per year up to two years in advance.

  10. Click OK to close the Holiday Schedule Details dialog.
