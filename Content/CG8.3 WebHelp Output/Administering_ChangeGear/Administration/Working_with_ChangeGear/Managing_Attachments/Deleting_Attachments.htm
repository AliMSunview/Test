Deleting Attachments

You can delete attachments you have mistakenly attached, or files that have become obsolete, erroneous, etc.

To remove an attachment:

  1. In the CI form, click the Attachments tab.

  2. In the Attachments list, select the attachment(s) you want to delete.

  3. In the Attachments toolbartoolbar, click Remove.

  4. Save your changes to the CI form.
