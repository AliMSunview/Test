Reviewing Attachments

To review an attachment:

  1. In the CI form, click the Attachments tab.

  2. In the Attachments list, double-click on the attachment you want to review,

    - OR -

    Click on the attachment to select and click ViewView.

ChangeGear downloads the file and attempts to open it in its associated application.
