Managing Attachments

ChangeGear lets you attach support documents to your items, such as deployment documents or installation files to your resources.

By managing attachments, you’re building efficiency into your system. Within each resource, you can provide all of the support documents needed for any type of work. Instead of searching through obscure directories, your staff and others can find the needed documentation directly with the resource.

The Attachments tab lets you add, remove, and review files. You can make the following adjustments to the columns in the Attachments list:

  • Move the columns

  • Adjust the column widths

  • Filter the columns

  • Sort the columns
