Service Level Agreements

A Service Level Agreement (SLA) is a special type of contract that can be between either external organizations or internal departments. For example, your organization may have an SLA with your ISP or your IT department and Accounting department may have an SLA about the availability of the accounting software.

SLAs give concrete goals that all parties agree upon. This alleviates misunderstandings that may arise from missed deadlines or servers undergoing excessive maintenance. ChangeGear can associate SLAs directly with the affect CI to keep the information where it will be needed.
