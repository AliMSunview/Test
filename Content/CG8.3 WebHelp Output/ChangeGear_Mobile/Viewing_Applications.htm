Viewing Applications

Your organization may have a preferred set of smartphone apps that it uses. The Applications page lists a collection of apps specified by your organization in alphabetical order. If the app is installed on your device, tapping on the app's icon launches the app. Otherwise, tapping the app icon on an Android device opens the app's website in your device's browser, while tapping the icon on an iOS device opens your device's app store.
