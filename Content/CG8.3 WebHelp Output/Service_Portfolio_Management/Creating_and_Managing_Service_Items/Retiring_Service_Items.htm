Retiring Service Items

After a service item has reached the end of its useful life span, you can retire it. Retired service items remain in ChangeGear for record-keeping purposes. Retired services can also be sent back through the Design and Review states if the service is ever re-activated.

To retire a service item:

  1. Navigate to the Service Portfolio tab and open the service item. Operational and Published services cannot be retired. The service must be Deactivated before it can be retired.

  2. Click Retire.
