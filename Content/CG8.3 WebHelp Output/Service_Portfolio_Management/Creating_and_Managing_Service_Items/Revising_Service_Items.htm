Revising Service Items

The revise action returns a service item to the Design state. This allows you to update information on the service item.

To revise a service item:

  1. Navigate to the Service Portfolio tab and open the service item.

  2. Click Revise (this option is only available in some service states). The service item changes to the Design state.

  3. Revise the service item. See Creating and Editing Service Items for more details.
