Reviewing Service Items

Newly designed or re-designed service items must be reviewed before they can be published.

To review a service item:

  1. Navigate to the Service Portfolio module and select a service item to review.

  2. Review the service item.

  3. Click Approve if the service item meets your organization's standards and can be implemented as designed.

  4. Click Revise to return the service item to the Design state.

  5. Click Retire if the item was previously in operation, but should now be retired and no longer be offered.

  6. Click Reject if the service item has never been in operation, and no further work is planned for the item.
