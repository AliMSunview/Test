Using the Service Portfolio Group Menus

Categories

Categories are used in the Service Catalog to group similar or related service items, such as Laptops or Training Classes. All categories are grouped into 2 service types: Business, Technical, and IT.  Categories can be added by clicking the +Add button at the top of the left column in the Service Portfolio. To edit a category, hover over the category row and then click the pencil icon.

Other

The Other group menu links to important areas not immediately accessible from the main ChangeGear Web interface, such as the Service Catalog Portal, Announcement Portal and the Self-Service Portal. Some items on this menu may be unavailable depending on your version of ChangeGear.
