Installing the ChangeGear Mobile App

ChangeGear provides an Android and iOS app for staff users and end users. Users can create new tickets, review existing tickets, browse Knowledge Base (KB) articles, and read announcements directly from their smartphone or tablet.

To install the ChangeGear mobile app on your Android or iOS device:

  1. Open your Android or iOS device's respective app store.

  2. Search for ChangeGear.

  3. Install the app on your device.

  4. Once the installation process completes, launch the app from the app store or from your device's app launcher.

For more information on using the ChangeGear app, please see the Using the ChangeGear Mobile App chapter of the ChangeGear User's Guide.
